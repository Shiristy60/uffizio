About us

Uffizio India is a daughter company of Uffizio GmbH, Berlin, Germany which operates since 1995. It was established in India year 2000 with the purpose of developing software for the European market. Thereafter, the company focused on own production and decided to develop the advanced featured softwares which they planned to sell globally in B2B business. To till date Uffizio is running successfully worldwide and providing advanced cloud-based software on international level.
